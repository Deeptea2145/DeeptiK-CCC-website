The Process

How It Works

Prevent Waste

Start by reducing food waste at the source. Plan meals, store food properly, and only buy what you need.

Donate Scraps

Collect fruit peels, vegetable scraps, and coffee grounds. Drop them off at your nearest CCC hub.

We Compost

Our partners transform your scraps into rich compost using community-scale composting equipment.

Soil Returns

Finished compost returns to local gardens, urban farms, and community green spaces around Long Island.
